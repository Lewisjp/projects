How to choose a Sweet Pumpkin
Pumpkin Pie Recipes


After coming up with a design, you're going to want it to come to life. Unlike a tattoo you can always get another pumpkin, but just like a tat, that modification is hard to change back.



Here are the steps to a perfect Jack-o'-Lantern:
  1. Create an outline of your design
    • Pencil is recommended
  2. Carve along your outline
    • Be sure to also make a lid at a 45 degree angle
    • Serrated knives work best for sawing through pumpkin skin
    • and always have parental supervision of course....
  3. Empty pumpkin guts into a bowl for later use
  4. Scrape the guts along the walls with a spoon
  5. Place a small candle whose flame won't touch the sides in the pumpkin
  6. Put the lid back on
